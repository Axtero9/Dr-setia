Our Story

A Patel Nagar practice, built one patient at a time.

Dr. Setia's Dental Centre opened its doors in 2002 with a simple idea: dental care should feel personal, not clinical. More than two decades later, that hasn't changed — many of our patients are now bringing in their own children for checkups.

The clinic is led by Dr. Sumeet Setia, an implantologist with 22 years of experience, alongside Dr. Ritu Verma Setia, a general dentist with 17 years in practice. Together they've built a calm, well-equipped space just off East Patel Nagar's main road, near Hotel Siddharth.
